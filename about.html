OUR STORY

Opened in 2023, Co Fitness was established with the goal of providing an exclusive technology gym experiencein the heart of Makati. Here at Co Fitness, we've revolutionized how gyms help their members reach theirfitness goals with the help of technology.

From the latest TechnoGym machine lineup to the acclaimed InBody 270 fitness assessment, we're constantlyproving how technology can not only assist but enhance our members' fitness. Together with our certifiedtrainers and fitness experts, we form a team dedicated to becoming your go-to gym in Makati for scientific andholistic fitness development.
